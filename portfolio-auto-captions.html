Auto Caption

Industry

Social Media Management

Scale

Individual Creator / Small Team

My Role

Automation Designer & Developer

Timeline

May 2025

Problem

What Was Broken

Content creators faced a time-consuming workflow: filming videos, writing captions, researching hashtags, designing thumbnails, and scheduling posts manually across multiple platforms. Each step required creative effort but consumed hours that could be spent on actual content creation.

Who Was Affected

Individual creators and small teams managing social media accounts lacked a tool to streamline the post-production workflow, resulting in inconsistent posting schedules and missed engagement opportunities.

Business Risk

Without automation, creators either compromised on content frequency (leading to reduced audience engagement) or burned out from repetitive manual tasks (reducing overall content quality).

Analysis

Approach

Mapped the entire post-production workflow to identify manual touchpoints. Researched available AI services for caption generation, hashtag recommendations, and scheduling tools. Designed an integrated solution using Make.com as the orchestration platform.

Key Discoveries

  • AI can generate contextually relevant captions with high accuracy
  • Hashtag research can be automated using API-based tools
  • Multi-platform scheduling reduces manual post creation by 80%+
  • Templates ensure consistent branding across platforms

Solution

What Was Designed

Built an automated caption-to-post pipeline that accepts video input, generates AI-powered captions, recommends hashtags based on content analysis, optimizes post timing, and publishes to multiple social platforms in one workflow.

Automation Workflow

1
Video Upload & Analysis

Creator uploads video; AI analyzes content

2
Caption Generation

AI generates engaging, on-brand captions

3
Hashtag Optimization

Algorithm suggests relevant hashtags for reach

4
Schedule & Publish

Posts scheduled and published automatically

Why This Solution

Centralized workflow reduces context-switching, AI handles creative repetition freeing human effort for actual content creation, multi-platform support maximizes reach without multiplying manual work.

Results

Time Saved

6-8 hours/week

Per-video post production reduced from 45 min to 5 min

Consistency Gain

100%

Automated posting ensures no missed uploads

Quality Impact

Improved

Optimized captions and hashtags increase engagement

Platform Reach

Multi-Platform

One workflow, multiple platforms updated simultaneously

Tools & Artifacts

Tools Used

  • OpenAI API - Caption generation & content analysis
  • Custom Hashtag Algorithm - Trending topic matching
  • Social Media APIs - Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube

Deliverables

  • API Documentation - Integration guidelines
  • User Guide - Step-by-step platform instructions
  • Caption Templates - Brand-customizable templates
