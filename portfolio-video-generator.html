Video Generator

Industry

Content Creation / Social Media

Scale

Individual / Small Business

My Role

AI Workflow Designer & Developer

Timeline

April 2025

Problem

What Was Broken

Content creators struggled to quickly turn ideas into engaging videos. Manual editing, asset collection, and publishing were slow and repetitive.

Who Was Affected

Solo creators and small businesses with limited resources for video production and editing.

Business Risk

Slow production cycles led to missed trends and reduced audience engagement, limiting growth potential.

Analysis

Approach

Mapped the video creation workflow, identified bottlenecks, and researched AI tools for image and video generation. Designed an automated pipeline using Make.com and AI APIs.

Key Discoveries

  • AI can generate story-driven images from prompts
  • Automated video assembly saves hours
  • Database storage enables easy content reuse
  • Ready-to-publish output streamlines social posting

Solution

What Was Designed

Built an AI-powered pipeline that generates images from prompts, assembles them into videos, stores all assets, and outputs ready-to-publish content.

Automation Workflow

1
Prompt Analysis

AI interprets the story and generates image prompts

2
Image Generation

AI creates images for each story step

3
Video Assembly

Images and clips are combined into a video

4
Publish

Final video is ready for social media

Why This Solution

Automation accelerates content creation, reduces manual effort, and enables creators to keep up with trends and audience demand.

Results

Time Saved

4-6 hours/video

Video creation time reduced by 80%

Content Output

Doubled

More videos published per week

Quality

Consistent

AI ensures on-brand visuals

Reuse

Easy

Assets stored for future projects

Tools & Artifacts

Tools Used

  • Make.com - Workflow orchestration & automation
  • AI Image Generator - Prompt-based image creation
  • Video Assembly Tool - Automated video editing
  • Database Storage - Asset management

Deliverables

  • Make.com Scenario - Video automation blueprint
  • Integration Docs - API and workflow documentation
  • User Guide - Video generator usage instructions
  • Video Templates - Customizable for different stories
